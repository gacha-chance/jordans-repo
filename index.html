What is a Gacha Game?

Gacha is a mechanic found in games, where you buy lootboxes and win random prizes. The word 'gacha' originates from 'gachapon,' which are vending machines that dispense capsule toys, commonly found in Japan! Gacha prizes are appealing in games because the best rewards can improve your gameplay, such as having a stronger character, or to fulfill your collector's desire. How Does Gacha Chance Work?

With Gacha Chance, there are three variables to control:
